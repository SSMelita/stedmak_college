Our Courses

Upgrade your skills with these courses

HOSPITALITY AND MANAGEMENT

  • COURSE OVERVIEW:
    • Explore the dynamic world of hospitality and develop essential management skills.
    • Learn about hotel operations, event planning, customer service, and marketing in the hospitality industry.
    • Understand the principles of effective leadership and team management.
  • TARGET AUDIENCE:
    Individuals interested in pursuing a career in hospitality management or related fields.
  • WHAT YOU'LL LEARN:
    • Hotel operations and front office management.
    • Event planning and management.
    • Customer service and guest relations.
    • Marketing and branding in the hospitality industry.

DRIVING LESSONS

  • COURSE OVERVIEW:
    • Learn how to drive safely and confidently with our comprehensive driving lessons.
    • Understand traffic rules, road signs, and defensive driving techniques.
    • Develop essential skills for parking, highway driving, and handling various road conditions.
  • TARGET AUDIENCE:
    Individuals who want to learn how to drive or improve their driving skills.
  • WHAT YOU'LL LEARN:
    • Understanding traffic rules and regulations.
    • Defensive driving techniques.
    • Parking and maneuvering in different scenarios.
    • Highway driving and long-distance travel.

ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING

  • COURSE OVERVIEW:
    • Get a comprehensive understanding of electrical engineering principles and practices.
    • Learn about circuit analysis, digital electronics, power systems, and more.
    • Develop practical skills in designing, installing, and maintaining electrical systems.
    • Explore emerging technologies and their applications in the field of electrical engineering.
  • TARGET AUDIENCE:
    • Individuals interested in pursuing a career in electrical engineering or related fields.
  • WHAT YOU'LL LEARN:
    • Principles of electrical circuits and components.
    • Digital electronics and logic design.
    • Power generation, transmission, and distribution.
    • Electrical machines and control systems.
    • Safety practices and regulations in electrical engineering.

CARPENTRY

  • COURSE OVERVIEW:
    • Learn the fundamentals of carpentry and develop skills in woodworking and construction.
    • Understand different types of tools, materials, and joinery techniques used in carpentry.
    • Build practical projects such as furniture, cabinets, and wooden structures.
    • Gain knowledge of sustainable practices and safety measures in carpentry.
  • TARGET AUDIENCE:
    • Individuals interested in pursuing a career in carpentry or woodworking.
  • WHAT YOU'LL LEARN:
    • Basic woodworking techniques and tools.
    • Understanding different types of wood and their applications.
    • Joinery methods for constructing furniture and structures.
    • Reading and interpreting blueprints and construction plans.
    • Safety practices and guidelines in carpentry.

MASONRY

  • COURSE OVERVIEW:
    • Gain expertise in the field of masonry and learn the art of building with bricks, stones, and concrete blocks.
    • Understand different types of masonry structures and construction techniques.
    • Develop skills in mortar mixing, bricklaying, and building walls, arches, and other masonry elements.
    • Learn about safety practices and codes related to masonry construction.
  • TARGET AUDIENCE:
    • Individuals interested in pursuing a career in masonry or construction.
  • WHAT YOU'LL LEARN:
    • Types of masonry materials and their properties.
    • Masonry tools and equipment.
    • Brick and block laying techniques.
    • Mortar mixing and application.
    • Structural principles in masonry construction.

PLUMBING

  • COURSE OVERVIEW:
    • Become proficient in plumbing techniques and learn to install, repair, and maintain plumbing systems.
    • Understand different types of pipes, fittings, valves, and fixtures used in plumbing.
    • Gain knowledge of plumbing codes, regulations, and safety practices.
    • Develop skills in troubleshooting plumbing issues and performing basic repairs.
  • TARGET AUDIENCE:
    • Individuals interested in pursuing a career in plumbing or those who want to acquire plumbing skills for personal use.
  • WHAT YOU'LL LEARN:
    • Plumbing system components and their functions.
    • Installation and maintenance of pipes, fixtures, and fittings.
    • Reading and interpreting plumbing blueprints.
    • Troubleshooting common plumbing issues.
    • Plumbing safety practices and regulations.
